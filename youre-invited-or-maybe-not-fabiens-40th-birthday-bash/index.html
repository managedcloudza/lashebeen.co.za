🎉🥳 You're Invited... or Maybe Not! Fabien's 40th Birthday Bash! 🥳🎉

Ladies, gents, and those who may or may not be on the guest list – hold onto your party hats, because something fabulous is brewing! That's right, the elusive Fabien, our culinary genius, is turning the big 4-0, and you might (or might not) be invited to the shindig of the century!

Event Details:

📆 Date: Saturday, 4th November 2023 at 18:00

🕺 Dress Code: Well, darlings, wear whatever tickles your fancy. Whether you're rocking your grandma's sequin dress, a three-piece suit, or a onesie, you do you. We don't judge; we celebrate!

🍹 Food & Drinks: Of course, we've got you covered with the finest food and cocktails this side of the moon. Fabien's in charge, after all! But hey, we're not opposed to donations for the Shebeen bar. It's like bringing a bottle of wine to a wine tasting, only classier.

The Address: A Little Secret

Now, for the secret handshake... drumroll, please... we won't tell you the address. That's right, you'll have to be a real-life Sherlock Holmes to find us! Invitations, like treasure maps, will be sent via direct message to the chosen few.

But What if You Don't Get an Invite?

If you didn't receive a direct message with the address, well, bless your heart, you might not be on the list. But don't fret! It just means you haven't unlocked the secret code to the party of the year. Keep rubbing those four-leaf clovers, maybe next time you'll get lucky!

Now, let's be honest – this party is the kind of event you'll tell your grandkids about. It's an exclusive soirée, where good times and laughter are mandatory. Fabien's 40th is bound to be legendary, a bit wild, and absolutely unforgettable.

So, in conclusion, you might be invited, you might not. But if you are, congratulations! You're in for a night to remember. And if you're not, well, there's always next time. Cheers, fabulous friends, and here's to Fabien turning 40 in style! 🎂🎈 #FabienTurns40 #LaShebeenSecrets
